About GOAL 15 LIFE ON LAND


First, whether we live in a city or a desert, all human lives benefit from nature
such as forestes, rivers, and forests.

In order for living things, including humans, to be able to live on earth for a long
time, it is necessary to protect natural envoirment and biodiversity.

There are many things that humans can do.

For example,

  • Reduce DEFORESTATION and plant more trees.
  • Combat DESERTIFICATION.
  • Protect and preserve ECOSYSTEM.
  • Reverse LAND DEGRADATION.
  • End threats to ENDANGERED SPECIES.
  • And protect BIODIVERSITY.

Q. What can we do to help achieve this goal?

  1. Recycle used paper products.
  2. Adopt sustainable lifestyles and reduce the burden on forests and ecosystems (e.g. use eco bags, promote energy conservation).
  3. Donate to support forest and wildlife conservation organizations.

There are so many things that we can do to achieve SDGs. Let's do something small to contribute to the SDGs.
